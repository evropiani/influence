The whole game in 10 seconds

You have influence. It buys land, one point per cell. Land and nodes make more influence. Hold the most ground when the clock stops — or be the last one standing. That's it. Everything below is spice.

Learn the basics
Controls
PC
  • Click toward where you want to grow.
  • Drag to pan · wheel to zoom · WASD works too.
  • 1234 toggle Wall / Farm / Outpost / Bomb.
  • Ctrl+click also paints walls, old-school.
Mobile
  • Tap toward where you want to grow.
  • Drag to pan · pinch to zoom.
  • Tools live in the bottom bar.
  • Tap the minimap to jump around.
Growing & fighting

Empty land costs 1 influence per cell. Enemy land costs more the longer they've held it:

Brightfresh · soft · cheap
Darkdug in · tough · pricey

The Commit slider sets how hard each tap swings — low for careful pokes, high to throw everything at one push.

Hit the bright seams, don't grind into dark cores. And pushing far from your nodes costs extra — capture your way forward instead of lunging across the map.

Nodes & your base

Nodes are the ringed dots on the map. Grow over one to capture it. Each node pays you influence, raises your cap, and extends how far you can push cheaply. Stealing an enemy's node pays a fat bonus and starves them.

The star is your base. Lose it and your income drops 30%. To rebuild: fully entrench a node (it'll glow), tap it, pay 300. Back in business.

Barriers — the map fights back

Grey rock barriers carve up the map — you can't own them or cross them, so armies flow around like water. New barriers keep forming mid-round. A lane that's open now might not stay open. Plan accordingly.

Your tools
1 Wall · 20

A wall has 25 durability. Attackers must chip through it before they can take the cell — every point of influence they throw at it knocks off a point of durability, and the damage shows as cracks.

Top walls: fresh. Bottom walls: one hit from crumbling.

  1. Press 1 (or hold Ctrl) and click cells inside your territory to paint walls.
  2. Wall the chokepoints — gaps between barriers, the ring around your base and farms. Don't wall everything; 20 each adds up.
  3. Watch for cracks on your own walls and re-wall behind them before they break.
2 Farm · 400

Pays +120 influence every 15s and raises your influence cap by 40. It pays for itself in under a minute — the earlier you build it, the more it prints.

  1. Press 2, then click a cell deep inside your territory.
  2. Build the first one as soon as you can spare 400 without stalling your expansion.
  3. Protect it — if the cell is captured, bombed or crushed, the farm is gone.
3 Outpost · 300

Every 12 seconds it claims up to 40 cells around itself, preferring enemy land, leaving a defended beachhead and softening nearby entrenchment. It's also a forward supply point: you can expand (and bomb) cheaply around it, the same as around a node.

The triangle is the outpost — see the red splash chewing into teal land.

  1. Press 3, then click a cell at your frontier, facing the enemy.
  2. Let it grind: it erodes their dark, entrenched cells that would cost you a fortune to take by hand.
  3. Chain outposts forward to extend your reach across the map.
4 Bomb · 250

An aimed strike. Blasts a crater out of enemy territory — their cells, walls and buildings are wiped (nodes survive). Works anywhere within your supply range, on a 25s cooldown.

Orange ring = valid target. Red ring = out of range or still recharging.

  1. Press 4 — a reticle follows your cursor.
  2. Aim at a dark, dug-in blob or a farm cluster and click. The crater is neutral ground: cheap to walk into.
  3. Outposts extend your supply range — an outpost + bomb combo reaches deep behind enemy lines.
Game modes — tutorials
Classic

The clock race: most territory when time runs out wins.

  1. Spawn smart (10s): pick a spot near 2–3 nodes, away from rivals.
  2. First minute: capture the nearby nodes — income, cap and reach all come from them.
  3. Build a farm once you can spare 400. It pays for itself in ~50 seconds.
  4. Mid game: push bright seams, wall your chokepoints, plant an outpost toward your main rival.
  5. Last minute: territory only counts at 0:00 — dump your whole bar, bomb the biggest enemy blob, and grab everything you can.
Battle royale

No clock. Lose all your land and you're out — last one standing wins.

Greyed-out names in the scoreboard are already eliminated.

  1. Survive first, win later. You can't be voted out of a clock — only starved to zero cells. Don't overextend early.
  2. Spawn away from the crowd and let the bots maul each other.
  3. Build a tight economy: nodes, farms, and walls around your core.
  4. Strike players who just lost a fight — their fresh land is bright and cheap.
  5. When someone's eliminated, their leftover ground is free real estate. Snowball it.
The Zone

A circular map that collapses five times, two minutes per phase — and never quite toward the middle.

Scorched ground outside the circle is gone for good. The red ring is the next safe zone.

  1. The red ring appears 20 seconds before each collapse and shows exactly where the map shrinks to.
  2. Crushed nodes are deleted — their income and cap vanish from whoever owns them. Don't build your economy near the edge.
  3. When the ring appears, move immediately: push territory into the ring, abandon what's outside.
  4. Getting crushed to zero cells eliminates you, same as royale — the zone kills more players than the players do.
  5. After the fifth collapse: a 60-second final countdown — most ground inside the last circle wins.
Teams — 2v2 & 3v3

Bot allies who share your colour against a matched enemy team. Most combined ground wins — or wipe the other team out.

One colour per team — your allies are literally on your side of the map.

  1. Pick 2v2 or 3v3 on the start screen (the opponents field is fixed in team play).
  2. You can't hurt teammates — expansion, bombs and outposts all spare them. Push through the gaps they leave.
  3. Ally nodes and outposts extend your supply range — expanding along your ally's territory lets you strike deep.
  4. Pincer: split an enemy between you and an ally and eat both flanks at once.
  5. The result screen scores by team totals — a fed ally wins games too. Don't hog every node.

Cheat sheet

  • Grab nearby nodes first. Always.
  • Bright enemy edges = free real estate. Dark cores = money pit.
  • Farm early, outpost forward, bomb the stubborn bits.
  • Watch for cracks — yours and theirs.
