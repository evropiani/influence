Start in 20 seconds

You spend influence to claim land — one point per cell. Capture nodes (the ringed dots) to earn more of it. Whoever holds the most ground when the timer ends wins.

  1. Pick your spawn when the round starts — near a couple of nodes, away from rivals.
  2. Tap toward where you want to grow. Push at enemies, roll over nodes to capture them.
  3. Once your economy is steady, spend on walls, farms, outposts and bombs from the bottom bar (keys 1–4).

That's genuinely all you need to play. Everything below is reference — open a section only when you want the detail.

The basics
Land, influence & nodes

Each cell you own trickles a little income. Nodes pay far more, raise your spending cap, and let you push out further for less. So the loop is simple: grab nodes → earn influence → claim land → grab more nodes.

Empty land costs 1 per cell. Enemy land costs more the longer they've held it:

Brightfresh · soft · cheap
Darkdug in · tough · pricey

Hit the bright seams; don't grind into dark cores. Two catches keep a lead honest: pushing far from your own nodes costs extra, and once you already dominate the map, new land costs a little more still — your supply lines are stretched. A commanding lead still wins; it just can't steamroll on autopilot.

The Influence / click slider (bottom bar) sets how much of your influence each tap spends — low for careful pokes, high to throw everything at one push. Tap Q/E to step it mid-fight.

Your base & barriers

You start with a base (the star). Lose it and your income drops 30% until you rebuild one: hold a node until it's fully entrenched (it starts to glow), tap it, and pay 300. Your own base has a bright, pulsing ring so you can always find it.

Grey rock barriers can't be owned or crossed — territory flows around them like water. Some are there from the start; new ones keep drifting in mid-round (after a calm opening), so a lane that's open now might close later.

Controls
PC
  • Click toward where you want to grow.
  • Drag to pan · wheel to zoom · WASD too.
  • 1234 select Wall / Farm / Outpost / Bomb.
  • QE lower / raise influence-per-click.
  • M toggles sound · ☰ Menu quits to the menu.
  • Ctrl+click also paints walls.
Mobile
  • Tap toward where you want to grow.
  • Drag to pan · pinch to zoom.
  • Tools live in the bottom bar.
  • Tap the minimap to jump around.
Tools — spend from the bottom bar
Wall · 20Blocks attackers, 25 durability. They chip through it before taking the cell — damage shows as growing cracks. (1 or Ctrl+click to paint.)
Farm · 400Your economy engine: +120 influence every 15s and +40 cap. Build it deep in safe territory; it pays for itself in under a minute.
Outpost · 300Every 12s it claims up to 40 nearby cells, preferring enemy land, and acts as a forward base you can expand and bomb from. Chain them toward a rival.
Bomb · 250Aimed strike: craters enemy land, walls and buildings in your supply range (nodes survive). The crater burns 10s — they can't retake it, you can. 25s cooldown.
Game modes
ClassicThe default. Most territory when the 5-minute timer ends wins.
Battle RoyaleNo clock. Lose all your land and you're out — last one standing wins.
King Of The HillHold more than half the golden centre zone; that builds your timer. 60 cumulative seconds wins (getting knocked off pauses it, doesn't reset it).
DominationA race — first to 75% of the map wins. Turbo economy: bombs recharge in 5s, farms pay +250, outposts fire every 6s.
The ZoneA circular map that collapses 5 times toward a random spot (a red ring warns you 20s ahead). Crushed land and nodes are gone for good. Most ground in the final ring wins.
Teams 2v2 / 3v3Bot allies share your colour — no friendly fire, shared supply. Most combined ground wins, or wipe the other team out.

On the start screen you set opponents (0–99), difficulty (Easy / Normal / Hard) and the mode — Classic is selected by default; the rest sit under "More modes".

Play with a friend
Play with a friend · P2P

Up to 4 humans in one match (you + 3 friends), plus bots — or none at all for a pure friend fight. Your browsers connect directly to each other: no server, no accounts. Works in every mode except Teams.

  1. Host: under "Play with a friend", press Host. A short 6-character code appears — send it to your friend over any chat app.
  2. Friend: press Join, type that code, press Connect. You're in — no reply needed.
  3. Host: press Add Player for each extra friend (up to 3), then pick the mode, difficulty and bot count and press Start. Everyone chooses their own spawn.

If two players pick the same colour, one is changed automatically. After a round the host presses Play again to rematch — no reconnecting. (If the code service is ever unreachable it falls back to longer copy-paste codes: you send an invite code, your friend sends a reply code back, you paste it. Some strict corporate/mobile networks can block the direct connection.)

Quick tips

  • Grab the nearest nodes first — always. They fund everything.
  • Bright enemy edges are cheap; dark cores are a money pit — go around them, or bomb them open.
  • Farm early on safe ground, outpost toward the enemy, bomb the stubborn bits.
  • Watch for cracks on walls — yours and theirs.
  • In a timed game, territory only counts at 0:00 — spend your whole bar in the last few seconds.
