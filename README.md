## ΏθΟψ The whole game in 10 seconds

You have **influence**. It buys land έΑΦ one point per cell. Land and captured **nodes** make more influence. Hold the most ground when the clock stops (**Classic**) or be the last one standing (**Battle royale**). Everything else is spice.

- **Bright** enemy land is fresh, soft, and cheap to take. **Dark** land is dug in and expensive.
- Once you already dominate the map, new land costs a little more per cell έΑΦ **supply lines stretch**, so a lead has to be re-earned instead of steamrolling itself. Still hard; just no runaway blowouts.
- Nodes ΏθΟψ pay income, raise your influence cap, and extend your reach έΑΦ and they **level up** the longer you hold them (up to +60% income). Steal an enemy's for a fat bonus, bigger if it's levelled.
- Grey **rock barriers** έυε carve up the map έΑΦ impassable, and new ones form *mid-round*. No sniping across open ground.
- Lose your **base** έφΡ and your income drops 30% until you rebuild.

## ΏθΣξ Multiplayer (P2P)

Up to **4 humans** in one match (host + 3 friends), plus bots έΑΦ or **zero bots** for a pure friend fight. The browsers connect **directly to each other**, no server, no accounts. Host presses **Host** and gets a **6-character code**; each friend presses **Join** and types it in έΑΦ then the host hits **Add Player** for the next one and starts the game. Full flow on the [Tutorial page](https://evropiani.github.io/influence/tutorial.html).

**Short codes:** with an optional lightweight signaling relay configured, invites become **6-character codes** the friend just types in (otherwise the long copy-paste codes are used). The relay only holds a connection handshake for a few minutes and deletes each code the moment it is used έΑΦ no game or personal data.

## ΏθνΨ Modes & bots

- **Classic** έΑΦ most territory when the clock runs out wins.
- **Battle Royale** έΑΦ no timer, last one standing wins.
- **King Of The Hill** ΏθΣΣ έΑΦ one **golden zone** at the map's center. Hold more than half of it to build up **60 cumulative seconds** of control and win. Everyone fights over the same ground.
- **Domination** ΏθγΑ έΑΦ a race: **first player to 75% of the map wins**. Bombs on a 5s cooldown, farms pay +250, outposts fire every 6s. Pure aggression.
- **The Zone** ΏθΦ┤ έΑΦ a circular map that collapses in **5 phases** (2 min each) toward a **random** spot έΑΦ a red ring warns you 20 seconds ahead. Whatever falls outside is crushed, *including nodes* έΑΦ and their cap and income die with them.
- **2v2 / 3v3** έγΦΎ╕Π έΑΦ bot allies in **your colour**. No friendly fire, shared supply range, most combined ground wins.
- **Difficulty** έΑΦ Easy / Normal / Hard. Harder bots think faster, commit harder, and build farms and outposts of their own. Up to **99** opponents, if you're feeling brave.
